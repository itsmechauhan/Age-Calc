What is an Age Calculator?

An age calculator is a simple tool that helps you calculate your exact age based on your date of birth. It shows your age in years, months, days, hours, minutes and even seconds. Everything runs inside your browser, so your data never leaves your device — making it 100% safe.

How Does This Age Calculator Work?

This calculator uses your selected date of birth and subtracts it from the current date and time. It automatically calculates time differences and converts them into all units such as years, months, weeks, days, hours and seconds.
